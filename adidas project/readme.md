🏢 Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of Adidas using Power BI. The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that help decision-makers improve business performance and strategy.

📌 Business Requirements

Through this Power BI-driven analysis, Adidas aims to empower its decision-makers with data-driven insights, fostering strategic growth and competitiveness in the dynamic sports and athletic industry.

Key Objectives:
Enhanced understanding of sales dynamics and performance drivers
Identification of geographical areas with high and low sales potential
Insights into product performance for better inventory and marketing decisions
Informed pricing and margin strategies to improve profitability
Actionable recommendations to optimize overall sales and profit
⚠️ Problem Statement

The main objective of this project is to analyze Adidas sales data and derive meaningful insights using key performance indicators (KPIs).

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Sales Analysis
Understand the overall sales performance of Adidas over time
Profitability Analysis
Evaluate the total profit generated across different dimensions
Sales Volume Analysis
Analyze total units sold to understand product demand
Pricing Strategy
Determine the average price per unit to assess pricing effectiveness
Margin Analysis
Evaluate average margins to measure profitability
📈 Chart Requirements

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

Total Sales by Month (Area Chart)
Visualizes monthly sales trends to identify peak periods
Total Sales by State (Filled Map)
Displays geographical sales distribution across regions
Total Sales by Region (Donut Chart)
Shows contribution of each region to total sales
Total Sales by Product (Bar Chart)
Compares sales performance across different products
Total Sales by Retailer (Bar Chart)
Highlights contribution of different retailers
🎯 Project Outcome

This dashboard helps:

Identify top-performing regions and products
Improve pricing and sales strategies
Support data-driven business decisions
Increase overall profitability
